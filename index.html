Welcome to Psistaria Tani Kola, a charming restaurant located in Himare, Albania. Situated near the sparkling waters of the sea, our restaurant offers a unique dining experience infused with the flavors and warmth of Albanian hospitality.

At Psistaria Tani Kola, we are passionate about serving delicious traditional Albanian cuisine crafted with care and expertise. From succulent grilled seafood to hearty meat dishes and fresh salads, our menu is designed to delight every palate.
